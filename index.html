About Our Practice

We provide quality dental care for the whole family. Your beautiful smile is our number one priority. We have a team of experienced dentists and staff who are dedicated to providing excellent dental care. The therapy equipment at our clinic is state-of-the-art so that any treatment provided at our clinic is accurate, quick and painless. We provide standard checkups and general procedures including hygiene, simple restorative procedures such as fillings, through to specialized care including implant placements and orthodontic treatments. Another advantage is that we subscribe to ongoing learning and professional development which gives our patients access to state of the art dentistry. We strongly embrace clients directed care where you, the client, are at the center of the healthcare process getting a treatment that is satisfactory with your health status.
